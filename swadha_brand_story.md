# Swadha - Brand Story & Overview

## The Origin Story

**Swadha** is a jewelry brand born from the creative vision of two talented women, Sudha and Swapni. The name itself represents their partnership - combining the first syllables of **Swa**pni and the last syllables of Su**dha**.

### The Team

**Sudha** (21) - Co-Founder | The Artisan
- Bachelor's in Fashion Technology
- Pursuing Master's in Fashion/Business Management
- Full-time dedicated to Swadha
- Expertise: Product pricing, sales, inventory management
- The night owl of the team with strong management skills

**Swapni** (22) - Co-Founder | The Executor
- Bachelor's in Computer Science Engineering
- Officially employed full-time elsewhere, but treats it like part-time
- More dedicated to Swadha than her day job
- Expertise: Sales, inventory, business operations
- Fast learner with untapped technical potential

**Surya** (24) - The Digital Troubleshooter
- Data Analyst/Engineer by profession
- Bachelor's in Electronics & Communications Engineering
- Skills: Python, Web Development, Digital Marketing, Data Analytics
- Role: Website development, tech infrastructure, digital strategy
- No equity stake - supporting the founders' dream

**Meghan** (26) - The Guy Who Makes Things Happen
- Bachelor's in Electronics & Communications Engineering
- Works in family ed-tech business
- Expertise: Productivity, management, social media
- Role: Social media management, stall bookings, finance & legal coordination
- No equity stake - supporting the founders' dream

**Ownership Structure:**
- 50% Sudha
- 50% Swapni
- Surya & Meghan: Supporting partners (no equity)

## Product Lines

### Swadha Bangles (Core Product)
- **Handmade authentic thread bangles**
- Custom designs created by Sudha and Swapni
- Labor-intensive, artisanal products
- Lower profit margins due to handcrafted nature
- Unique selling point: Authenticity and craftsmanship

### Fashion Jewelry Collection
Wide variety of accessories including:
- Rings
- Chains
- Bracelets
- Hair Accessories
- Charms
- Earrings

**Materials:**
- 18K Gold Plated
- Enamel
- Stainless Steel
- Oxidized jewelry

**Customer Feedback:** "Crazy collections - hard to choose just 1 or 2 products!"

## Future Vision

### Online Expansion
Moving to e-commerce through Shopify to:
- Reach wider audience
- Scale beyond offline constraints
- Build brand presence online
- Streamline operations

### Dual-Brand Strategy

**Brand 1: Swadha (Handmade Focus)**
- Authentic handmade bangles
- Artisanal products
- Heritage and craftsmanship focus

**Brand 2: Tivaleo/Ruvvier by Swadha** (Name TBD)
- Fashion jewelry line
- Curated accessories
- Modern, trendy designs
- Higher profit margins

**Rationale:**
- Differentiate handmade vs. curated products
- Target different customer segments
- Build distinct brand identities
- Flexible pricing strategies

## Business Philosophy

**What We Do:**
- Create beautiful handmade bangles
- Curate high-quality fashion jewelry from trusted retailers
- Offer wide variety with careful quality control

**What Sets Us Apart:**
- Personal touch in handmade items
- Carefully selected collections
- Quality over quantity
- Strong family partnership and values

**Target Audience:**
- Women seeking unique, quality jewelry
- Customers who appreciate handcrafted items
- Fashion-forward individuals looking for variety
- Those who value authenticity and good design
